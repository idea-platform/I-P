Programs

Innovation and Entrepreneurial Certificate

This program is made through a partnership with the UPRM Center for Business Development. It empowers engineering students to become business savvy and learn about innovation management directly from the experts. Talks and/or workshop are given by field experts, entrepreneurs and professors covering important topics about innovation that include: Design Thinking, Business Model Canvas, Value Proposition, Product Market Fit, among other.

Lean Startup Mentorship Program

This program provides students the necessary tools and mentorship to develop their startup ideas from scratch. These ideas turn into new companies, services, non-profit organizations and even programs for the student community. The selected disruptive thinkers develop their disruptive innovation management skills through a set of stages that prepares them towards competing in entrepreneurial competitions as well as pitching their ideas to successful entrepreneurs and investors.
