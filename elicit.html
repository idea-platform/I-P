Programs

Content Creation

Students in the Content Creation squads are encouraged to develop their communication skills by brainstorming new ideas and working in teams to implement their vision for new media content.

Social Media Marketing

Our Social Media squad is focused on growth hacking, community building, and viral marketing strategies. Members are challenged to use data analysis and creative marketing strategies to increase the amount of followers and engagement in our social networks.

Full Day Conferences

Idea Platform is known for organizing the best conferences to inspire and educate the student community. Our biggest conferences have been Elicit-Innovation and TEDxUPRM. During these conferences, we get the chance to engage with the student community and “infect” them with our movement of innovation!
